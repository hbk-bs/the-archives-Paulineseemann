In Reflection

Starting with limited digital basics in my life, I am positively surprised by my current level of knowledge, reflecting on the entire semester. Step by step, we started programming, first with HTML, then with CSS, p5.js and partly C++ in the end. It wasn't always easy, as we had to learn completely new language systems, but it was very motivating to see visual results straight away. I didn't start my studies at the HBK knowing that I was about to learn how to programme, but I'm now happy to have had and continue to have the opportunity to learn how to design digitally. I have learnt a lot about programming languages in general, e.g. how the computer receives, evaluates and executes commands and how precise the commands or algorithms sometimes have to be. I also learnt about several specific programming languages and their tasks, e.g. when creating a website. I also gained an insight into digital colour systems and the structure of simple electronic devices. Apart from the digital basics, I also feel more confident in working with digital media in general, feel more confident and see many advantages of using digital resources in design or combining analogue and digital aspects.

by Pauline Seemann

Digital basics, Visual Communication, HBK Braunschweig
